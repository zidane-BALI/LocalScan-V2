# LocalScan-V2
LocalScan V2 is a web-based prospecting and business discovery tool powered by the Google Maps API. It allows users to search for businesses anywhere in France by selecting a custom location, search radius, and business category (restaurants, real estate agencies, hotels, shops, and more).

The application automatically retrieves key business information, including company name, address, website, phone number, ratings, and other available details. An integrated lead management system enables users to organize prospects by assigning custom statuses such as Interested, Contacted, Not Interested, or Follow-up Required, making customer relationship management more efficient.

Designed for sales prospecting and local market analysis, LocalScan V2 combines Google Maps services with an intuitive interface to simplify business discovery and lead qualification.

Features
Search businesses anywhere in the world
Custom search radius
Multiple business categories (restaurants, real estate, hotels, shops, etc.)
Google Maps API integration
Business details (address, website, phone number, ratings)
Lead management and prospect qualification
Custom prospect status tracking
Responsive and user-friendly web interface
